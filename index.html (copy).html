Empowering Youth •Creating Opportunities • Building Legacy • Driving Innovation

Bathu is building a sneaker brand that Africans can proudly affiliate with. We believe in creating world-class sneakers, sustainable jobs, and re-igniting hope in people.

Founded in 2015 by Theo Baloyi, Bathu is more than just a sneaker brand—it's a movement that embodies African pride, resilience, and innovation. Today, Bathu has expanded to over 30 stores across South Africa and recently opened its first international store in Namibia.

Beyond fashion, Bathu is committed to social impact through initiatives like "Bathu for Batho," aiming to donate one million pairs of shoes to underprivileged schoolchildren. With a focus on creating sustainable jobs and reigniting hope, Bathu continues to inspire individuals to walk their own journey with confidence and pride.

At Bathu, we believe that every pair of sneakers tells a story—a story of perseverance, hope, and the relentless pursuit of dreams. We are not just selling shoes; we are inspiring individuals to walk their own journey with confidence and pride. Our commitment to excellence and service continues to drive us forward, as we strive to make a meaningful difference in the lives of our customers and communities.
